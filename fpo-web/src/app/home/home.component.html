Forestry Worker Job Placement

This form is for forestry workers in northern-interior British Columbia. We will match workers’ needs and goals to services and jobs.


Time: The average time to complete the application form is about 5 - 10 minutes. At this time, will need to complete your application in one session. This service will not save your information.

Instructions: Complete the form and press submit to send your application to our Forestry Job Placement Support Team. We will contact you as soon as possible. Please note it may take up to 2-3 weeks for us to contact you. We will process applications in the order they are received.

Privacy Protection:
The personal information on this form is collected by the Ministry of Labour for the purpose of Forestry Worker Job Placement Program under the authority of section 26 (C) and 27 (2) of The Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act. Questions about the collection or use of this information can contact Arthi Reddy, Forestry Worker Job Placement Program, 778-698-1635.
